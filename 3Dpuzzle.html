Concept

This is a 3D puzzle toy for kids. It's a toy for children to learn about teamwork. The main idea of this project is to catch the interest from the children and the parents.
Clients want us to display the Safety and Happiness in this video, so we add diverse motions to increase the qualities for the project.

Film

The most difficult part was to guide the kids to do the right step at the time and keep them stay in front of the camera. We set a main camera at the front and had 3 teams being responsible for different tasks. One of us guided the children and control the time. The others filmed the details of the kids.

Edit

I edited the video based on the script and add cute music. Motion graphics for the descriptions and titles were made uniquely and would shit with the titles. I also used colorful shapes to rich the visual experience.
