Concept

This project was a function introduction video of health care products. The video was used in a seminar to introduce and convince customers that their products were great.

Script

The main task I did was making a presentation video and adding many texts on each page was required. Typesetting was important and I tried to control word numbers on each page to let the audience have a good visual experience.

Motion

The content of the narration was the main template followed when designing the film. I used Illustrator to make the action script before starting the work. This phase could make the motion graphic work more organized for me.
